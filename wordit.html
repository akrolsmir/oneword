How the game works:

1) {{ room.currentRound.clueGiver }} picks a random phrase, and writes a clue related to the phrase
2) Every other player writes a decoy phrase they think also matches the clue
3) Every other player tries to guess {{ room.currentRound.clueGiver }}'s real phrase among the decoys

You earn points if:

* You correctly guess {{ room.currentRound.clueGiver }}'s phrase among other players' decoys
* Other players pick your decoy-- the more players you trick, the more points
* Bonus points if no one correctly guessed {{ room.currentRound.clueGiver }}'s phrase (their clue was too generic, or the decoy(s) were all too good!)

Players take turns as the clue giver in order. The game ends after every player has been clue giver 5 times, and the player with most points wins!
